Finding Value in Lending Club Data

Current State of Consumer Loans

Companies like Lending Club, Prosper and Upstart offer a new market for individual investors to invest. Lending Club releases its data on issued loans for each year and for my project I am exploring Lending Club's data from 2007-2013, the last year in which loans issued have been paid off. With this, I observed the set of all the loans features and attributes and whether or not they were paid off.

Initial Findings

fig 1: A disproportionate number of loans for renewable energy uses defaulted, regardless of credit rating

fig 2: Mortgage holders were also more likely to default than renters, possibly due to the collapse of the housing market and the extra debt load that mortgages bring.
